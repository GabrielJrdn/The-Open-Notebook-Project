About The Open Notebook Project

The Open Notebook Project is a collection of shared GoodNotes documents that everyone can contribute to. Find shared notebooks by other people and edit them together. Upload your own and wait for the world to join. You can use it to play games like Tic Tac Toe together, make lists, share your sketches, create a public database of flash cards, and more.
